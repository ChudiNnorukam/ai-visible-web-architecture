# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This repository exists to document a real system clearly and rigorously.

Participation here should improve the quality of the public artifact. That means:

- be direct, specific, and respectful
- critique claims, evidence, and implementation details rather than people
- prefer reproducible proof over status arguments
- keep discussions aligned with the repository's documented evidence boundary

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable in issues, pull requests, discussions, or email about this repository:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- deliberate misrepresentation of evidence or authorship
- spam, trolling, or low-signal promotional activity
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

The repository owner is responsible for interpreting and enforcing this policy.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that conflict with this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to `hello@chudi.dev` with:

- a short description of what happened
- links or screenshots when available
- the impact on the project or participants

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.
