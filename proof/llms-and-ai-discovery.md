# LLM and AI Discovery Proof

`chudi.dev` publishes explicit AI discovery surfaces that help search and AI systems understand site identity, usage preferences, and content access paths.

## Observable Elements

- `llms.txt` for site-level discovery and priority pages
- `llms-full.txt` for expanded retrieval context
- `ai.txt` for agent-facing guidance
- `.well-known/llms.json` for machine-readable metadata

## Architectural Role

These files are part of the machine-readable discovery layer. They do not replace the website. They make the website easier for AI systems to interpret consistently.
